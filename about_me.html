Who's "Me?"

Hi. My name's Mohib, and I'm a junior in AHS, taking Computer Science II. I'm 15 years old, and I was born in Toronto, Canada, where I would then move throughout various cities in New Hampshire, before settling in Allen, Texas. When I started school in AHS, computer science wasn't a subject that I'd highly involved myself in, but if you were to somehow recover all the thumb drives and files I've made, used, and lost, there'd be a considerable amount of mainly useless programs, small text-based games, and even mods of games created by friends.

What makes a portfolio important?

My digital portfolio's important to me because with it, I can create a catalog, which will allow me and other people to easily navigate throughout my past work and find an exact record of a project, assignment, or other type of media. Additionally, if a skill of mine becomes rusty, or I become unfamiliar with a certain topic, I can easily revisit it through this portfolio, and revitalize my memory of the concept at hand.
