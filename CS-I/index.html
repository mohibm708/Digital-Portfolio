Major Topics

An excerpt from Mr. Ben-Yaakov's syllabus describes the focal points of Advanced CS-I as:

  • Number systems and conversions
  • Boolean algebra and logical operations
  • Computer architecture
  • Input and output
  • String manipulation
  • Scope
  • Data types and their digital representation and conversions
  • Mathematical, relational, logical, and assignment operators
  • L-values and R-values
  • Sequential, branching, and iterative solutions
  • Analysis, design, coding, testing, and debugging
  • Algorithm design and implementation
  • Arrays
  • Structures and classes
  • Random numbers
  • Digital citizenship
